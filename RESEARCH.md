## Caveats

- Most menu/review sites (Yelp, Grubhub, DoorDash, WhereYouEat, SinglePlatform) block automated
  fetching, so **exact item-level prices could not be scraped**. All menu prices on the site are
  clearly labeled as illustrative placeholders; the owners should replace them with the real menu.
- Directory listings disagree slightly on ZIP (11223 vs 11204); 11223 appears on the majority of
  listings and matches the Kings Hwy address, so the site uses 11223.
