👨‍👩‍👧 Family-run, Brooklyn-proud

Our little corner of Kings Highway

A juice bar and a Mexican kitchen under one roof — because breakfast deserves fresh fruit and dinner deserves birria.

The story

Two crafts, one counter

Most juice bars close by afternoon. Most taquerias don't open until lunch. We do both, seven days a week from 6 AM to 9 PM — the first fresh-squeezed juice on the block in the morning and hot, made-to-order Mexican food all day.

Everything is hecho a mano: tortillas pressed by hand, birria braised slow until it falls apart, fruit cut fresh every morning. Nothing sits under a heat lamp, nothing comes out of a bottle.

We cook like it's for our own family — because it is. That's why neighbors tell reviewers things like “great food and service, all prepared fresh and to order.”

A day at the counter

6 AM to 9 PM, every day

🌅 6:00 AM — First press

Oranges get squeezed, fruit gets cut, the desayunos griddle heats up. Commuters grab jugo verde before the train.

🌮 Midday — Lunch rush

Tacos, tortas and platillos out of the kitchen made to order. The birria consomé has been simmering since morning.

🥭 Afternoon — Snack o'clock

Mangonadas, fruit cups con tajín and esquites for the after-school crowd.

🌙 Evening — Dinner & delivery

Platillos and family orders, dine-in or delivered on DoorDash, Grubhub and Seamless until 9 PM.

Questions people ask

Quick answers

Do you really open at 6 AM?

Yes — every day, including weekends. Juice, coffee and desayunos are ready from the minute we unlock the door.

Can I customize my juice or smoothie?

Absolutely. Pick any fruits or veggies from the counter and we'll press or blend them to order.

Do you deliver?

Yes — find us on DoorDash, Grubhub and Seamless, or call (718) 975-4480 for pickup.

Is there seating?

Yes — dine in or grab a spot at our outdoor seating when the weather's good. Takeout is always fast.

Do you take cards?

Yes, cards and cash both work.
